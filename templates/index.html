About Us

Haidar Educational and Welfare Trust is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and skill development initiatives.

Registered: Indian Trust Act, 1882

Status: Government Recognized

Transparency: Publicly Accountable
